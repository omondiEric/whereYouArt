Overview

Have you walked into an art museum, look at an exhibition, and wondered what other artworks have been displayed in this gallery? How did past curators make use of the space? What if you can view the past and the present at the same time?

Design Process

The design of Where You Art was achieved through an iterative process, which started by carrying out contextual inquiries by speaking to three participants. We then carried out an analysis of the tasks that had been identified for the application. We then used sketches and storyboards and paper prototypes to think about a design approach that would support the tasks. An important part of the whole process was the usability tests, that helped ensure the design we came up with was actually usable in a real world setting.

Solution

An augmented reality mobile app that allows users to view past museum shows and interact in the present space.
