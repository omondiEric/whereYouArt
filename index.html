Overview

Have you walked into an art museum, looked at an exhibition, and wondered what other artworks have been displayed in the gallery? How did past curators make use of the space? What if you can view the past and the present at the same time?

Design Process

Where You Art is an innovative product made for museum visitors. Our team observed, listened, and asked questions. We built a prototype and used feedback from user tests to refine our design through many iterations. Learn more about our entire design process

Solution

An augmented reality mobile app that allows users to view past museum shows and interact with them in the present space.
